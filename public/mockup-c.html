Context: the company and my role

CreditorWatch is an Australian B2B credit risk intelligence platform. When I joined, the core product represented ~70% of revenue but was showing cracks. Churn was rising, competitive pressure was mounting, and the internal strategy was doubling down on high-level portfolio reporting — an expensive bet I was brought in to pressure-test.

"Too much noise, not enough signal."

Users weren't asking for fewer alerts. They were asking for clearer meaning.

My approach: diagnose before build

Dashboard audit findings and behavioural data

Rather than solutioning, I ran a structured discovery program. Alert email click-through sat at 4%. Users were exporting to Excel to do work the platform should have done for them.

Jobs-to-be-Done Framework

01 · Awareness

Is this alert worth my attention?

02 · Investigation

How does this risk affect my actual exposure?

03 · Actioning
